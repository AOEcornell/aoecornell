Philanthropy

Our communities have given so much to us, everything from education to public parks, so it's time for us to give back. We plan outreach initiatives for the local Ithaca area, either through community service or organizing our own events. Each recruitment class chooses a different philanthropic arc to be their focal point for the semester and that semester's philanthropic initiatives.

Sisterhood

We all come from different backgrounds, grew up in different places, and are studying different majors. This sorority is a chance for us to all bond together, despite those differences, as fellow women in STEM. Studying for finals, attending campus events, celebrating holidays and special occassions, and even just hanging out: these are all different ways that the sisterhood of A立E develops.
